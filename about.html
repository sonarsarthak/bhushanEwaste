Company Profile

Pioneering Eco-Friendly Recycling Solutions

Discover our story, mission, core values, and credentials as Nashik's premier government-authorized electronics recycling firm.

Our Story

Founded in Nashik, Maharashtra, **Bhushan E-Waste Recycling Pvt. Ltd.** was established with a singular vision: to address the growing environmental threat posed by obsolete electronics. Over the years, we have grown from a local collector to a state-authorized recycling plant serving corporations, banks, healthcare providers, and local government departments nationwide.

We believe that standard business practices must align with environmental preservation. Through advanced technology, certified segregation, and data-destruction pipelines, we guarantee that no collected devices end up in public landfills.

Nashik's Pride

Operated locally under strict regulatory frameworks to deliver pan-India environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To deliver transparent, secure, and environment-friendly disposal services that help corporations offset their carbon footprints and legally dismantle obsolete IT assets with 100% trace credentials.

Our Vision

To build a complete circular economy framework in India, where electronic waste materials are constantly harvested, recycled, and returned to production loops to achieve zero landfill targets.

Our Core Values

The principles driving our operations, client interactions, and regulatory adherence daily.

Integrity & Trust

Operating with full transparency by providing verifiable audit trails, certifications, and direct facility visits.

Circular Economy

Recovering secondary materials like copper, iron, aluminum, and plastics to feed manufacturing loops sustainably.

Strict Compliance

Strictly complying with all state and central pollution regulations, maintaining flawless compliance records.

Our Recycling Journey

Tracing our development and operational highlights over the years.

2018 — The Foundation

Inception of Bhushan E-Waste Recycling in Nashik with a basic collection storage warehouse and 4 local collection routes.

2020 — Government Authorization

Acquired central pollution license and MPCB state authorization certificates. Commenced commercial collection routes across Nashik, Pune, and Mumbai.

2022 — ITAD Expansion

Expanded services to incorporate secure corporate IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) and built our high-grade hard drive shredding and degaussing unit.

2025 — Automated Sorting Facility

Inaugurated our new semi-automated electronic sorting and eco-recovery plant, boosting recycling limits to 12,000+ tons per annum.
