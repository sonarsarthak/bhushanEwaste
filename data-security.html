Destruction Methodologies

We deploy mechanical and electromagnetic processes depending on media formats.

🧲

Electromagnetic Degaussing

Degaussers expose magnetic hard disk drives (HDDs) to a powerful 20,000-gauss pulse. This scrambles recorded magnetic domains instantly, rendering sector data unreadable. Degaussed disks cannot be reformatted or used again.

  • ✓ Ideal for standard magnetic platter HDDs
  • ✓ Destroys internal read heads during treatment
  • ✓ Completely silent operation
⚙

Mechanical Shredding

Solid-state drives (SSDs) and storage cards do not rely on magnetic loops and cannot be degaussed. Instead, they pass through our mechanical shredder, which grinds silicon chips into 2mm granules.

  • ✓ Required for flash storage arrays & SSDs
  • ✓ Physical destruction ensures zero recovery
  • ✓ Scraps are sorted to extract precious metals

Chain of Custody Pipeline

Protecting data assets requires a secure chain of custody. Bhushan maintains control of files from collection to shredder exit:

  • ✓ Doorstep Serialization: We log each storage drive's barcode serial number before leaving your office.
  • ✓ Locked Transport Cartridges: Drives are sealed in locked steel transit carts.
  • ✓ Real-Time Transit GPS: Shipping vehicles are monitored live by our security team.
  • ✓ CCTV Record Logs: Plant destruction feeds are recorded and kept for audit purposes.
