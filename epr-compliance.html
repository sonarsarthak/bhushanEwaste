Extended Producer Responsibility Rules

Fulfill your CPCB e-waste obligations. Explore compliance requirements, portal filings, target audits, and legal forms.

What is EPR?

Under the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2022, established by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC), manufacturers, importers, and brand owners (Producers) are legally required to recycle a defined percentage of the electronics they introduce to the Indian domestic market.

Producers meet these obligations by purchasing recycling credits from CPCB-registered electronic recyclers. Bhushan E-Waste Recycling processes e-waste and issues official, audit-ready recycling credits that fulfill your targets.

How to Achieve Portal Compliance

  1. Register on CPCB: Producers must register their company profiles on the online CPCB portal, declaring their equipment categories and import metrics.
  2. Calculate Annual Targets: The portal automatically determines your annual recycling target (measured in metric tons) based on previous sales data.
  3. Buy Credits from Bhushan: Bhushan collects and recycles equivalent electronic waste streams, converting the tonnage into official credits transferrable to your CPCB profile.
  4. File Annual Reports: Complete and submit annual returns (Form 2 and Form 6) before the government deadline to prevent regulatory fines.
