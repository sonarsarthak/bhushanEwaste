Advanced Processing Machinery

Our plant is configured with mechanical setups to recover raw metals cleanly.

Secure Shredding Units

We deploy high-torque, slow-speed mechanical double-shaft shredders. These setups slice computer towers, monitors, and casing boards into uniform pieces to isolate base metals from plastic layers.

⚙
High-Torque Dual Shaft Grinder

Processes up to 1.5 tons of plastic and steel casing scrap per hour.

⚙
Magnetic Separator Belts

Extracts iron and steel automatically from the output stream.

Sanitization & Refining

Our clean rooms host multi-pass magnetic degaussers and chemical copper recovery baths, supporting secure ITAD and industrial battery processing protocols.

⚙
HD-5 Degausser Unit

Wipes magnetic hard disk platters cleanly via 20,000-gauss pulses.

⚙
Lithium Neutralization Cells

Neutralizes trace organic solvents from disassembled battery nodes.

Secure Logistics Fleet

  • ✓ Real-Time GPS Tracking: Enterprise clients can access vehicle location updates.
  • ✓ Trained Security Personnel: Logistics crews are certified in safe transport and data custody.
