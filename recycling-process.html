The Recycling Flow

Step-by-Step Recycling Journey

Explore our complete chain-of-custody workflow from doorstep pickup to raw materials extraction and green certification.

01. Collection & Intake

Obsolete hardware items are cataloged at the client's site, scanned for serial tracking codes, and loaded into sealed shipping bins.

1

02. GPS-Tracked Transit

Locked logistics trucks transport collected electronic waste directly to our Nashik site, with GPS trackers providing real-time location security.

2

03. Data Wiping & Destruction

Storage media like hard drives and SSDs are isolated, degaussed using 20,000-gauss pulses, and physically shredded to protect corporate information.

3

04. Manual Segregation

Technicians separate chassis pieces, copper wire, silicon circuit boards, glass screens, and power supplies to prepare them for mechanical grinding.

4

05. Mechanical Refinement

Advanced grinders reduce remaining pieces to uniform granules. Magnetic separators and water tables isolate base metals (steel, copper) from plastics.

5

06. Fulfilling the Cycle

Raw metal scrap and plastics are supplied to licensed smelting plants to manufacture new hardware casings, avoiding landfills completely.

6

07. Compliance Verification

Audit logs are compiled on the portal. Brand owners and corporate clients receive official certificates of destruction and green recycling certificates.

7
