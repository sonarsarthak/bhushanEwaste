The Recycling Pipeline

Our clear operational pipeline from device collection to materials recovery.

1

Collection

Sealed shipping bins are loaded into company-owned GPS-tracked logistics trucks.

2

Sorting & Disassembly

Electronics are disassembled to separate metals, glass, wiring, and boards.

3

Crushing & Refining

Refined scraps are processed mechanically to extract gold, copper, and iron.

4

Certification

Clients receive audit-ready compliance certificates confirming responsible recycling.

Responsible Metal Recovery

Discarded electronic circuit boards contain high concentrations of valuable metals alongside toxic elements like lead and mercury. Our process ensures:

  • ✓ Pollution Prevention: Safe separation keeps toxic mercury out of local groundwater.
  • ✓ Authorized Traceability: Form 2 waste tracking documentation for business audits.
  • ✓ Circular Economy Support: Reclaimed steel and copper are returned to manufacturing loops.
