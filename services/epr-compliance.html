Frequently Asked Questions

Understand EPR portal target credits.

When an authorized recycler processes electronic waste, they generate credits. Producers buy these credits to fulfill their legal import targets.
CPCB manages credit transfers directly on the national online portal, verifying that credits match actual weighed recycling loads.
