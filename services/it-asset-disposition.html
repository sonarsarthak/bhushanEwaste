IT Asset Disposition

Secure & Compliant ITAD Solutions

Protect your corporate intellectual assets and ensure environment-safe retirement of obsolete server racks, workstations, and network switches.

Key ITAD Capabilities

Delivering full peace of mind through a comprehensive chain-of-custody tracking protocol.

✓

Certified Data Destruction

Complete magnetic degaussing and mechanical disk shredding backed by official certificates matching audit standards.

✓

Serialized Inventory Log

Barcode tracking of each system, scanning serial numbers at pickup, transport, sorting, and disposal stages.

✓

Maximum Asset Recovery

Extracting raw components (gold pins, copper wires, plastic enclosures) to feed them back to manufacturing chains.

ITAD Process Workflow

How we decommission and process corporate hardware safely.

1

Site Assessment

We audit hardware inventory at your location and estimate logistics requirements.

2

Safe Pack & Load

Devices are sealed in locking carts and loaded into company-owned GPS vehicles.

3

Data Sanitization

Storage drives undergo mechanical destruction or multi-pass sanitization.

4

Reporting & Certs

Official verification manifests and green certificates are shipped to your team.
